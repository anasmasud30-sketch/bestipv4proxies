What are Dedicated IPv4 Proxies?

In short, Dedicated IPv4 Proxies are private IPv4 addresses for your exclusive use. Their defining strength is no noisy neighbours, with the main trade-off being that they're pricier at large fleets.

How Dedicated IPv4 Proxies work

When you route traffic through dedicated ipv4 proxies, your request exits from one of the network's IPs and the destination sees that address instead of yours. The way those IPs are sourced is what sets dedicated ipv4 apart from other types.

Security & privacy

Use HTTPS so traffic stays encrypted through the tunnel, keep credentials out of shared code, and choose providers that source IPs transparently. These habits apply to dedicated ipv4 proxies as much as any type.

Mistakes to avoid

  • Ignoring data-expiry rules and losing gigabytes you already paid for.

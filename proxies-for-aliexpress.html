How to avoid bans on AliExpress

  • Keep one sticky IP per AliExpress account; never swap mid-session.

Account & fingerprint tips

On AliExpress, the IP is only half the story. Keep each account's browser fingerprint, time zone and behaviour consistent, and avoid logging many accounts from one device. An anti-detect browser plus a dedicated IP per account is the reliable combination.

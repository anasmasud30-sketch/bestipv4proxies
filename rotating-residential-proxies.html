What are Rotating Residential Proxies?

In short, Rotating Residential Proxies are residential IPs that change per request. Their defining strength is spreads load across many IPs, with the main trade-off being that they're individual IPs are short-lived.

How Rotating Residential Proxies work

When you route traffic through rotating residential proxies, your request exits from one of the network's IPs and the destination sees that address instead of yours. The way those IPs are sourced is what sets rotating residential apart from other types.

What are Anonymous (Elite) Proxies?

In short, Anonymous (Elite) Proxies are proxies that hide that a proxy is in use. Their defining strength is strong anonymity, with the main trade-off being that they're quality varies by provider.

How Anonymous (Elite) Proxies work

When you route traffic through anonymous (elite) proxies, your request exits from one of the network's IPs and the destination sees that address instead of yours. The way those IPs are sourced is what sets anonymous (elite) apart from other types.

Security & privacy
